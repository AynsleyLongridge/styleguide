Monogram to Visual Marque

The brand steams from my monogram, the encorperation of my initials ( AL ) interwoven together to create a seamless line. The initials are combined in a way that was subtle and minimalistic creating the core of the brand. From the monogram we can then see how the visual marque was devised through duplicating and connecting. The idea behind the marque is a lightening bolt, representing a striking brand.

Colour Scheme

The colour scheme is based around Prussian Blue, Cinnabar and Madang. The prussian blue acts as a blackdrop under the cinnabar and madang to highlight key areas and touchpoints. The Cinnabar will be used as the heading and body font colour and background to buttons.

Wordmark

My brand workmark is created with the typface Vanadine in the weight of bold. I wanted a sans serif type that linked with the brand in terms of how it would be striking and edgey. When using my name as a wordmark this can work with all three colours of the colour scheme depending on the use. This font is not to be used for any plain body text!

Revisiting the visual marque

  • Please leave padding of at least 20px around the visual marque to ensure breathing space.
  • Do not rotate or flip from the origional design.
  • The visual marque is developed from the monogram, do not use them together.
  • Keep the visual marque within the three colours from the brand palette

Typeface

I am using the font Glegoo because of its mordern elegance. This slab serif typeface can be easily read from small sizes whilst also holding its own elegance in headings. This is the only font to be used as paragraphs and headings in the brand
